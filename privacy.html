Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

At CySign, we value your privacy. Our app is designed to help users prepare for the Cyprus driving theory test without collecting or storing any personal data on external servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information such as names, emails, or locations.
  • No analytics or tracking data.
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers.

Information Stored Locally

All progress data (such as your test results, mistakes, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. You can clear this data anytime by uninstalling the app.

In-App Purchases

Any in-app purchases are processed securely by Apple or Google via their respective stores. CySign does not have access to or store any payment information.
